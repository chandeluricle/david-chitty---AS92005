WHATS RE-CREATE ABOUT?

Re-create is a fashion brand which started as a way to provide fair employement for women in Dey Tmey, Cambodia. The founder, Erica Gadsby created ReCreate along with her friend Marielle Van De Van in NZ, and so created ReCreate. Along with ethical work practices and providing opportunities for marginalised communities, they practice being environmentally friendly by using eco-friendly materials for their clothes

IMPORTANT MATERIALS USED:

As is with Verdure, we are focused on the environmental benefits of the clothing for this brand. ReCreate uses alot of different, natural materials, and strays very rarely on synthetic materials. These include organic cotton sourced from thailand (certified by the Global Organic Textile Standard, GOTS), GOTS certified 100% organic cotton, and sustainable stretch denim manufactured from 97% Better Cotton Initiative sourced cotton, sustainably milled using 100% recycled water. All these materials are incredibly sustainable and pose better alternatives to more common materials that are more wastful in water usage, use unethical and unsustainable cotton practices, and more which increase our carbon footprint. This brand is incredibly efficient in their usage of materials
