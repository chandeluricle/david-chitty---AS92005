WHATS EARTH RATED ABOUT?

Earth Rated is a brand founded in Canada which has the goal of making eco-friendly, thoughtfully designed products for dogs. They take great care in making everyday movements for people and their dogs simpler and better, and we chose them for their immense progress in making dog products environmentally friendly. It even is B-Corp certified, meaning it has reached the highest standards of environmental impact

RECOMMENDED PRODUCTS

These refill dog poop bags, though amazing in their own right, are also incredible in being eco-friendly. They're made from 65% certified post-consumer plastic, giving new life to waste that would've been thrown into the ocean as pollution, or even landfill

This is Earth Rated's Fly Toy. Every other current toy of Earth Rated is made from certified natural rubber, but for this specific toy it is made from thermoplastic elastomer, which though is plastic is significantly easier to recycle.

This is the 3 in 1 double coat shampoo by Earth Rated. It's made of a mixture between natural and synthetic ingredients, over 90% being natural. The packaging, as is the trend for Earth Rated products, is also environmentally friendly, being made of 25% post consumer materials
