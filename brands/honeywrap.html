WHATS HONEY WRAP ABOUT?

Honey Wrap is a New Zealand-made brand with a passion for reducing plastic use through eco-friendly materials. It purely uses natural materials for all its products, saving the equivalant of 75 metres of plastic wrap use over its lifetime. As their page says, "To date, Honeywrap customers have saved around 80 million metres of plastic wrap ending up in landfill, waterways, and our ocean". Their products include Honeywraps for wrapping food, beeswax candles, etc.
