"lets shop the green way, together"

Verdure's goal is to encourage eco-friendly shopping through uplifting our local eco-friendly brands and businesses. From fashion to beauty, we've selected a handful of brands who work their hardest to minimise their carbon footprint. In the battle against climate change, each of these small NZ businesses help you shop the green way, and we hope you find a product that'll help you with your eco-friendly journey.
